Frequently Asked Questions

Is this image compressor free to use?

Yes, our image compression tool is completely free to use with no hidden charges or watermarks.

What image formats are supported?

We support JPG/JPEG, PNG, and WebP formats for both input and output.

Is there a file size limit?

The current maximum file size is 10MB per image. For larger files, consider using professional desktop software.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No, all processing happens in your browser. We don't upload or store your images on any servers.

How much can I reduce my image size?

Compression results vary by image, but typical savings range from 30% to 80% without noticeable quality loss.
